What is Habit Stacking?

Habit stacking is a powerful strategy where you build new habits by "stacking" them onto existing ones. This makes the new habits easier to remember and maintain.

By focusing on just one new habit each week, you'll create a sustainable path to lasting change without feeling overwhelmed. Small steps lead to big results!

Example:

"While I wait for the kettle to boil, I'll stretch for 30 seconds."
